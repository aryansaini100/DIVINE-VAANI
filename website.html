Hindi: Shiv ji ko 'Mahadev' kaha jaata hai...

Hindi: Hindu dharm mein anek devta hain, jinmein har ek ka apna vishesh mahatva hai. Yeh sabhi Bhagwan vishwa ke niyam, shakti aur raksha ke roop hain.

English: Hinduism includes many gods and goddesses, each with specific powers and divine responsibilities that help maintain universal balance.

Bhagwan Shiv

Hindi: Shiv ji ko 'Mahadev' kaha jaata hai. Ve vinaashak hai jo andhakaar aur ahankaar ka ant karte hain. Unka trishul, damru aur tandav prasiddh hai.

English: Lord Shiva is the destroyer of evil and known as Mahadev. His symbols are the trident (Trishul), drum (Damru), and the cosmic dance (Tandav).

Vishnu Ji

Hindi: Vishnu ji palankarta hain. Unke dashavtar (10 avatars) mein Ram aur Krishna pramukh hain. Ve dharti par dharm ka palan karne aate hain.

English: Lord Vishnu is the preserver of the universe. His 10 avatars, including Rama and Krishna, restore balance and protect dharma on Earth.

Ram Ji

Hindi: Maryada Purushottam Shri Ram adarsh putra, pati aur raja ke roop mein jaane jaate hain. Unka jeevan Ramayan mein varnit hai.

English: Lord Ram is known as the ideal son, husband, and king. His life is a guide to righteousness, told in the epic Ramayana.

Krishna Ji

Hindi: Krishna ji ne Mahabharat mein Geeta ka gyaan diya. Ve prem, leela, ran-neeti aur bhakti ke devta hain.

English: Lord Krishna is the god of love, strategy, and devotion. He delivered the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita in the Mahabharata.

Hanuman Ji

Hindi: Hanuman ji bal, bhakti aur buddhi ke devta hain. Unhe Ram bhakt aur chiranjeevi maana jaata hai.

English: Lord Hanuman symbolizes strength, devotion, and wisdom. He is considered immortal and the greatest devotee of Lord Ram.

Durga Mata

Hindi: Maa Durga ko shakti ka roop maana jaata hai. Navratri ke dauraan unke 9 roop ki pooja ki jaati hai.

English: Goddess Durga represents divine feminine energy. She is worshipped in nine forms during Navratri.

Lakshmi Mata

Hindi: Lakshmi ji dhan, samriddhi aur sukh-shanti ki devi hain. Unki pooja Diwali par hoti hai.

English: Goddess Lakshmi is the goddess of wealth and prosperity. She is especially worshipped on Diwali.

Saraswati Mata

Hindi: Maa Saraswati vidya, sangeet, aur kala ki devi hain. Unka vahan hans hai.

English: Goddess Saraswati is the goddess of knowledge, music, and arts. She rides a swan and carries a veena.

Kali Mata

Hindi: Kali maa vinaash aur raksha ka roop hain. Ve adharma ka naash karti hain.

English: Kali is the fierce form of the goddess who destroys evil and protects the righteous.

Ganesh Ji

Hindi: Ganesh ji vighna harta aur buddhi ke devta hain. Koi bhi shubh karya unki pooja ke bina shuru nahi hota.

English: Lord Ganesha is the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom. He is worshipped at the start of every auspicious task.

English: The third eye (pineal gland) is linked to inner vision and spiritual power.
